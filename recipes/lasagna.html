Simple and Easy Lasagna

This lasagna is very easy to make and only takes around 20 minutes! Substitute out any ground beef to make it a vegetarian friendly recipe. Let me know how you all enjoy it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz tomato sauce
  • 15 oz shredded cheese
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
    •

    Instructions

    1. Cook the noodles until soft but not fully cooked through
    2. Create a layer of noodles in a 10x8 pan
    3. Place a layer of tomato sauce, cheese, and ground beef
    4. Repeat the layering process as needed
    5. Cook for 30 minutes at 400 degrees or until thouroughly cooked.
