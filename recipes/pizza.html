Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

The perfect pepperoni pizza recipe for anyone wanting an easy meal for dinner. Just 4 ingredients, don't let anyone call you lazy, you are RESOURCEFUL!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • pepperoni

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Lay out dough on a large pan
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough
  4. Place pepperonis around the dough
  5. Layer as much cheese as prefered
  6. Add more pepperonis as desired
  7. Cook for 15-20 minutes or until dough is thouroughly cooked
