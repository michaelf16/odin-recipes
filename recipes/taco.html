Taco Recipe

Crunchy and Delicious Tacos

My favorite taco! So simple and easy to mix and match any ingredients. Personally I like the works with as much crammed in as possible! One tip is while using a crunchy taco, place a soft taco on the plate to catch anything falling out the back. Then you get a soft taco after you are finished with the crunchy!

Ingredients

  • Hard Taco Shells
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomatos
  • Sour Cream
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the ground beef until brown
  2. Take the shells and fill them up!
  3. It really is that easy! Enjoy!
